Our Story

RapidFlow Plumbing & Drain has been solving plumbing problems for Tri-City homeowners since 2013. Here's what drives us and why our customers keep calling us back.

Our Story

Started With One Rule: Treat People Fairly

RapidFlow was founded in 2013 by Marcus Reid, a licensed journeyman plumber who spent a decade working for a large regional contractor; he grew frustrated watching customers get hit with hidden fees, vague quotes, and repeat visits for the same problems.

He started RapidFlow with a simple commitment: show up on time, give a real written price before touching anything, and fix it right the first time. Twelve years later, that's still how every job runs.

Today, RapidFlow employs a team of 8 licensed plumbers serving Kitchener, Cambridge, Waterloo, and the broader Tri-City region. Over 4,200 jobs completed. 400 Google reviews at 4.9 stars.

What We Stand For

The Principles Behind Every Job

These aren't statements on a wall. They're the rules Marcus built the company on, and every technician is trained to uphold them.

Honest Pricing

You receive a written quote before any work begins. No line items appear on your invoice that weren't on the quote. The price we give is the price you pay.

Reliability First

We show up when we say we will. For emergencies, that means within the hour. Consistent follow-through is how we've built 400 five-star reviews one job at a time.

Fix It Right Once

We don't patch problems temporarily. We diagnose accurately, use quality materials, and back every repair with a 12-month labour warranty. We intend to fix it correctly the first time.

Respect for Your Home

Our plumbers lay drop cloths, wear boot covers, and clean up completely before leaving. We treat your home the way we'd want ours treated. That's non-negotiable.

Licensed, Always

Every technician who enters your home holds a valid Ontario journeyman plumbing licence. We never use unlicensed sub-trades, on any job, at any price point.

Available When It Matters

Plumbing emergencies don't keep business hours. We answer the phone 24/7, dispatch immediately, and never charge extra for nights, weekends, or holidays.

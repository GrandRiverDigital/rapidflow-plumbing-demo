Our Story

Guelph's Plumber Since 2013

RapidFlow started as a one-truck operation with a simple promise: answer the phone, show up on time, and do the job right. Twelve years and thousands of satisfied customers later, that promise hasn't changed.

Today we're a team of licensed plumbers covering Guelph, Kitchener, Cambridge, Waterloo, Fergus, Elora, and Rockwood. But we're still locally owned, still personally accountable, and still the plumber your neighbours call first.

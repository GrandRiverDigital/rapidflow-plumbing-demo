Bathroom Renovation · Kitchener

Master Bath Plumbing Overhaul (1980s Build)

A 1980s semi-detached home in Kitchener needed a full master bathroom gut and replumb. The original galvanized supply lines were corroding, the tub valve was shot, and the homeowners wanted to add a double vanity and frameless shower. We replaced all supply and drain lines, installed new shutoff valves, roughed in the double vanity, and fitted a thermostatic shower valve.

Full copper supply line replacement

Burst Basement Pipe — Sunday Night, 60-Minute Response

A homeowner called at 10:30 PM on a Sunday after discovering water pouring from a split copper pipe in the basement utility room. We had a technician on-site by 11:25 PM. The damaged section was cut out and replaced with new copper, the water was restored, and the homeowner was back in bed before 1 AM. No overtime surcharge.

Water Heater Upgrade · Waterloo

Aging Tank to Tankless: Same-Day Installation

A family in Waterloo called Monday morning with no hot water; their 14-year-old tank had failed overnight. They asked about switching to tankless. We reviewed their gas line capacity, recommended an appropriate unit, and had a Navien tankless water heater installed and running by 2 PM the same day. The old tank was hauled away.

Old tank removed and disposed of
Navien tankless unit installed same-day
Gas line upgrade and commissioning
